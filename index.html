About the game

2048 is a 4x4 tile game created by web developer Gabriel Cirulli. The goal of the game is to combine numbers to create bigger number tiles in order to get to the magic number, 2048.

Rules

The rules of how to play 2048 are simple. You have four ways to move the tiles: left, right, up, and down. You combine numbers like 2 and 2 to make a 4 tile, 4 and 4 to make an 8 tile, and so on and so forth. A low number such as a 2 or 4 will appear in a random spot with every move you make. This makes the board fill up faster, and the rows more difficult to move as the game goes on.
